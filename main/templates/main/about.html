A simple, light-weight web application for accelerating communal kitchens. The Kitchen Accelerator is equipped with an arsenal of utilities such a hob-booking, meal-time estimates and fridge space allocation in a sleek interface. The app scales seamlessly kitchens of different sizes and eliminates copious amounts of stress when waiting for the time and space to prepare your meals.
